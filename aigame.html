How to Play

Goal: Be the first to play all your cards.


Matching: Play a card that matches the top card's color OR number/symbol.


🔴 Skip: Next player loses their turn.

🔄 Reverse: Direction of play reverses.

+2 Draw Two: Next player draws 2 cards.

🌈 Wild: Choose any color.

🌈 Wild +4: Choose any color AND next player draws 4.


UNO: Press the UNO button when you have 1 card left or draw a penalty!


Can't play? Draw a card from the deck. If it's playable, you may play it immediately.
